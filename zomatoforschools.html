Concept

overview

Scope

This is a concept for searching Schools and Colleges just like we search Restaurants in Zomato. Herein the user will get the list of Schools and Colleges according to the area he is living in. Also the user can search for Schools and Colleges according to any specific area they prefer. Moreover,the user can also Favourite his/her School or College and also view all the evenets that are happening in there.

RESEARCH

I spent a couple of hours understanding the problem domain. Found out how big the problem is, what are the current solutions available, and possible ways to mitigate this problem. I had to personally spent time interviewing the founder and the co-founders of the App initiative understanding the process in detail. This gave me insights I needed to define a solution.

IDEATION

Keeping the content strategy at helm of designing, I adopted a different approach and explored unusual navigation. Here, I focused on minutest of elements and movements because every action on website had to speak about the capabilities of Hakuna Matata.
