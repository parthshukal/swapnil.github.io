process

RESEARCH

I tried to play around the functionalities of the website, and was not quite impressed. So, I finally decided to use primary color as yellow to make the content and product look relatable. And to make it interesting and jazz up things I added a pinch of red and blue as secondary colors.

IDEATION

As I was not impressed by the functionalities as well, I went through few retail website and planned to add some minimal and significant functionalities. I added a page to feature the collections, latest offers and displayed similar product depending on what user is browsing. I also exhibited the services that can be provided by the retailer and categorized men’s and women’s footwear section.
